Motif-Z (short for Motif detection with statistical Z-Score) is a software tool to identify motifs from major histocompatibility complex (MHC) class I binding peptide sequence data. It is designed to analyze peptide sequences with specific or various lengths (from 7 to 14 amino acids). Although Motif-Z is developed as an sequence analysis tool for MHC binding peptides class I data but it can also be used efficiently in detection of motifs from short piptide sequences of various lengths (7mer to 14 mer).
